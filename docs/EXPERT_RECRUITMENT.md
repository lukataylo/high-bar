### Compliance & process notes (read before sending)

- **Draft-only.** Nothing here is sent automatically. The founder reviews and sends
  each message manually.
- **Honest framing.** Every draft states High Bar is a **new** expert network and
  that experts are **paid per accepted answer**, on their **own schedule**. No
  fabricated traction or false urgency.
- **No MNPI / confidentiality.** Outreach invites people to answer *opinion,
  analysis, and historical-context* questions — never to disclose material
  non-public information or breach employer/client confidentiality. This mirrors
  the compliance gating described in `docs/EXPERT_NETWORK_RESEARCH.md`.
- **Re-verify before sending.** Titles and employers change; confirm each person’s
  current role and preferred contact method on their public page before outreach,
